This site documents experiments, prompts, and playbooks for applying AI to software quality assurance.

Goals
- Share reproducible prompts and small scripts for QA teams.
- Record experiments, outcomes, and recommended follow-ups.
- Provide short tutorials and templates teams can copy.

Contribute
- Add new Markdown files to `use-cases/`, `prompts/`, or `_posts/` for blog entries.
